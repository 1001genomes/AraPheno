    Citing Phenotypes & Studies:

    Every phenotype and study respectively that has been published on AraPheno, gets a DOI number assigned. This DOI number is registered with DataCite. For citing a specific phenotype or study, just open the detailed view of the corresponding study or phenotype and click on the DOI link.
