    Citing Phenotypes & Studies

    To fully honor the work of the contributing authors, please use the Cite link at the respective individual study or phenotype views (see screenshot below):



    Every phenotype and study respectively that has been published on AraPheno, gets a DOI number assigned. This DOI number is registered with DataCite. After clicking on the Cite link you get a list of all associated publications. You can retrieve the citations in various different formats:
